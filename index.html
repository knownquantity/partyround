Party Round

Who//
A group of young and chronically online investors, builders and operators you've never heard of. By design. We stay off the main stage, plugged into the corners of internet culture where meaningful opportunities actually surface. We avoid the loud, clout-driven deals of the startup industrial complex and rely on word-of-mouth from the communities we trust.
What//
An investment vehicle backing early-stage teams in consumer AI, crypto, and hardware. We invest in projects reshaping internet culture - new social graphs, experimental networks, creative tools, primitives for human connection, and technologies that bridge the online and offline.
Why//
Institutional backing wrapped in party round simplicity. Unlike traditional party rounds with meaningless dilution and cluttered cap tables, we're sophisticated capital that moves fast and adds real value beyond the wire.
